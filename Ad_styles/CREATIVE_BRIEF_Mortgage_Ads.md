## 1. Project Overview

### Objective
Create a distinctive, scroll-stopping, conversion-focused ad creative system for a mortgage brokerage serving both residential and commercial clients.

### Key Requirements
- **Distinctive:** Stand out from traditional mortgage advertising (no stock photos, no generic blue corporate look)
- **Scroll-stopping:** Pattern-interrupt design that captures attention in crowded social feeds
- **Conversion-focused:** Clear hierarchy driving toward pre-approval/consultation CTAs
- **Meta-compliant:** Adherent to Special Ad Category rules (no specific rates, no discriminatory targeting cues)

### Style Direction
**"Soft Brutalism + 3D Clay"** - A hybrid approach combining:
- Bold, structured layouts with thick borders and high contrast (Soft Brutalism)
- Warm, approachable 3D illustrations with clay/plastic material feel
- Outcome-focused messaging emphasizing journey, not rates

### Why This Style
| Traditional Mortgage Ads | Our Approach |
|--------------------------|--------------|
| Stock photos of houses | Custom 3D illustrations |
| Corporate blue palette | Bold, distinctive colors |
| Rate-focused headlines | Journey/outcome messaging |
| Generic "Apply Now" CTAs | Specific "Get Pre-Approved" CTAs |
| Forgettable, blends in | Memorable, stands out |

## 2. Brand Positioning

### Brand Personality
```
PROFESSIONAL but APPROACHABLE
BOLD but TRUSTWORTHY
MODERN but NOT COLD
CONFIDENT but NOT AGGRESSIVE
```

### Brand Voice
- Clear and direct (no jargon)
- Supportive and guiding
- Confident but not salesy
- Human and relatable

### Key Brand Messages
1. "We simplify the mortgage process"
2. "Expert guidance every step of the way"
3. "Your homeownership journey starts here"
4. "Trusted by thousands of families"

### Competitive Positioning
Position as: **The modern, transparent alternative to traditional lenders**
- Not a faceless bank
- Not a pushy sales operation
- A trusted guide through a complex process

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Audiences

#### Residential - First-Time Buyers
- **Age:** 25-45 (note: cannot target by age in ads)
- **Mindset:** Excited but overwhelmed, need guidance
- **Pain Points:** Confusion about process, fear of rejection, rate anxiety
- **Motivation:** Achieving homeownership dream, building equity

#### Residential - Refinance
- **Situation:** Existing homeowners
- **Mindset:** Practical, looking to optimize
- **Pain Points:** Unsure if now is the right time, process seems complex
- **Motivation:** Lower payments, access equity, better terms

#### Commercial
- **Type:** Business owners, investors, developers
- **Mindset:** ROI-focused, professional
- **Pain Points:** Need speed, flexibility, expertise
- **Motivation:** Portfolio growth, business expansion

### Universal Emotional Triggers
- Security and stability
- Achievement and progress
- Trust and confidence
- Simplicity and clarity

## 4. Visual Style Guide

### Style Definition: Soft Brutalism + 3D Clay

#### Soft Brutalism Elements
```
BORDERS
- Thickness: 3-5px
- Color: Black (#000000) or brand dark color
- Style: Solid, no rounded corners on outer frames
- Usage: Frame key content blocks, CTAs, headlines

CONTRAST
- High contrast between elements
- Clear separation of content zones
- Bold color blocking

STRUCTURE
- Visible grid structure
- Intentional asymmetry within grid
- Stacked/layered content blocks

TYPOGRAPHY
- Oversized headlines
- Mixed weights (Ultra Bold + Regular)
- Tight tracking on headlines
```

#### 3D Clay Elements
```
MATERIAL
- Glossy plastic/clay finish
- Soft reflections (not mirror-sharp)
- Matte to semi-gloss surface

FORM
- Rounded, organic shapes
- Slightly oversized proportions
- Playful but professional

LIGHTING
- Soft HDRI environment lighting
- Warm color temperature
- Subtle ambient occlusion

SHADOWS
- Soft contact shadows only
- No harsh drop shadows
- Grounded but not heavy
```

## 5. Typography System

### Font Stack

#### Primary Headlines
```
FONT: Clash Display
ALTERNATIVE: Cabinet Grotesk, Space Grotesk
WEIGHT: Bold (700) to Black (900)
STYLE: Variable font preferred

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Size: 48-72px (mobile), 64-96px (desktop)
- Tracking: -2% to -4% (tight)
- Leading: 0.9-1.0 (tight)
- Case: UPPERCASE for impact, Title Case for longer phrases
```

**Download:** [Clash Display](https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/clash-display) (Free)

#### Secondary Headlines / Subheads
```
FONT: Satoshi
ALTERNATIVE: DM Sans, Inter
WEIGHT: Medium (500) to Bold (700)

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Size: 24-36px (mobile), 32-48px (desktop)
- Tracking: -1% to 0%
- Leading: 1.2
- Case: Title Case or Sentence case
```

**Download:** [Satoshi](https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/satoshi) (Free)

#### Body / Supporting Text
```
FONT: Satoshi or DM Sans
WEIGHT: Regular (400) to Medium (500)

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Size: 16-20px (mobile), 18-24px (desktop)
- Tracking: 0%
- Leading: 1.4-1.5
- Case: Sentence case
```

#### Trust Text / Small Print
```
FONT: Space Grotesk or Inter
WEIGHT: Regular (400)

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Size: 12-14px
- Color: 60-70% opacity or secondary color
- Usage: Disclaimers, reassurances, fine print
```

## 7. 3D Asset Specifications

### Technical Requirements

```
SOFTWARE: Blender (recommended), Cinema 4D, Spline
RENDER ENGINE: Cycles (Blender) or similar PBR renderer
EXPORT FORMAT: PNG with transparency (2K minimum, 4K preferred)
COLOR SPACE: sRGB
```

### Material Specifications

```
MATERIAL TYPE: Glossy Plastic / Clay

SHADER SETTINGS (Blender Principled BSDF):
- Base Color: [From palette]
- Roughness: 0.3 - 0.5 (semi-glossy)
- Specular: 0.5
- Subsurface: 0.1 (for warmth)

ALTERNATIVE APPROACH:
- Matte base with subtle gloss
- Think "high-quality toy" or "premium clay"
- NOT metallic, NOT glass, NOT hyper-realistic
```

```
HDRI ENVIRONMENT: Soft studio or outdoor
TEMPERATURE: Warm (5500K-6500K)

SHADOWS:
- Soft contact shadows
- No harsh edges
- Ground plane with subtle reflection optional
```

## 9. Copywriting Guidelines

### Meta Compliance: What You CANNOT Say

| DO NOT USE | REASON | COMPLIANT ALTERNATIVE |
|------------|--------|----------------------|
| "Rates as low as 5.99%" | Rate advertising restrictions | "Competitive rates for you" |
| "5.875% APR" | Specific rate claims | "Great rates available" |
| "Save $347/month" | Implies specific rate | "Potential monthly savings" |
| "Perfect for first-time buyers" | Age discrimination implication | "Ready to buy your first home?" |
| "Great for young families" | Age/familial discrimination | "For your family's future" |
| "In [specific zip code]" | Location discrimination | "Serving [broad region]" |
| "Low income? No problem" | Discriminatory targeting | "Options for every situation" |

### Approved Headline Formulas
