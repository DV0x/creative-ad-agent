## Target Audience / ICP

**Focus:** Derived from site analysis

**WHO:**
- Rate-conscious homeowners (30-55) approaching renewal or considering refinancing
- First-time buyers (25-40) intimidated by traditional bank processes, seeking guidance
- Self-employed/complex income earners (35-50) rejected or underserved by big banks
- Real estate investors and newcomers needing specialized mortgage products

**PAIN POINTS:**
- "I'm paying too much on my mortgage and leaving money on the table"
- "My bank rejected me because I'm self-employed / have non-standard income"
- "I don't know if I should renew with my current lender or switch—what if I make the wrong choice?"
- "Rates keep changing and I'm stressed about timing my mortgage decision"
- "I've called multiple lenders and gotten different answers—it's exhausting and confusing"
- "I don't trust my broker—are they pushing the product that pays them the most?"

**MOTIVATIONS:**
- Save thousands: Primary driver is financial optimization through lower rates
- Reduce stress: Want expert guidance to simplify complex decisions
- Get approved: Need flexibility when big banks say "no"
- Feel confident: Desire reassurance they're making the right financial move
- Save time: One-stop solution vs. calling multiple lenders
- Trust and transparency: Unbiased advice without hidden agendas

**LANGUAGE:**
- Speak in practical, outcome-focused terms: "save thousands," "stress-free," "best rate"
- Value transparency and simplicity over technical jargon
- Respond to guarantees and social proof (reviews, testimonials)
- Mix emotional (peace of mind) with rational (specific dollar savings) decision-making
- Use phrases like "no obligation," "free consultation," "unbiased advice" to reduce perceived risk
- Empowerment-focused: "take control," "shop around," "don't settle"

## Key Insights for Ad Creation

**Strongest Hook Angles:**

1. **Rate comparison urgency:**
   - "Paying 5.5% at your bank? See rates from 2.49% at Canada's largest broker"
   - Works for: Homeowners at renewal, refinancers

2. **Rejection reversal:**
   - "Self-employed? Investor? Newcomer? Get approved where banks said no"
   - Works for: Complex income situations, non-traditional borrowers

3. **Renewal timing:**
   - "Your mortgage renews in 120 days. Don't auto-renew without comparing rates"
   - Works for: Existing homeowners approaching maturity date

4. **Guarantee boldness:**
   - "Find a better rate anywhere. We'll beat it or give you $500"
   - Works for: Rate shoppers, skeptics, comparison seekers

5. **Stress/confusion relief:**
   - "Confused by mortgage options? Free expert advice, zero obligation"
   - Works for: First-time buyers, overwhelmed prospects

**Emotional Triggers to Leverage:**
- Loss aversion: "Stop overpaying" (fear of leaving money on table)
- Relief: "Stress-free process" (escape from confusion/rejection)
- Confidence: "Guaranteed best rate" (decision reassurance)
- Empowerment: "Take control of your mortgage" (vs. bank autopilot)
- Security: "16,000+ five-star reviews" (social proof reduces risk)

**Visual Opportunities:**
- Rate comparison calculator showing bank vs. True North savings
- Social proof badges: "16,000+ reviews" / "$33B arranged" / "19+ years"
- Teal brand color (stands out in blue-heavy mortgage vertical)
- Human broker faces (counter impersonal bank stereotype)
