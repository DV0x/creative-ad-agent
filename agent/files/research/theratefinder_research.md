## The Offer
AI-powered mortgage rate search and comparison platform offering residential, commercial, construction, and private mortgages across Canada. Access to 350+ lenders with 24-48 hour pre-approvals and rush closings. Rates from 4.19% for residential, up to 100% LTV for commercial, 72-hour funding for BRRR loans.

## Target Audience / ICP

**Focus:** Derived from site analysis

**WHO:**
- Real estate investors (fix & flip, BRRR, multi-unit) aged 30-55 seeking fast, flexible financing
- Self-employed professionals and business owners with complex income structures rejected by traditional banks
- Non-traditional borrowers: bad credit, bank declines, non-residents, foreign buyers
- Property developers and contractors needing construction or bridge financing with tight timelines

**PAIN POINTS:**
- "I got rejected by my bank despite having property equity and investment experience"
- "Traditional lenders take weeks - I need funding in days or I lose the deal"
- "Banks don't understand my income as a self-employed person or investor"
- "I need 100% LTV or specialty financing that A-lenders won't touch"
